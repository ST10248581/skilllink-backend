Skillink Help & FAQ

Your questions answered. Your freelance journey supported.

What is Skillink?

Skillink is a platform that connects clients with freelance opportunities within their loca area. Whether you're a designer, developer, tutor, or writer, Skillink helps you earn.

Who can use Skillink?

Skillink is built for:

  • People offering local freelance services
  • Clients seeking help

How do I create a profile?

Sign in with your Google account or fingerprint. Your profile is synced automatically. You can update your name, phone number, and payment methods in the Settings tab.

How do I get hired?

Add your services, respond to project requests, and maintain a strong rating by delivering quality work.

How do payments work?

Clients can add payment methods and pay freelancers directly. Skillink supports card-based payments and tracks transactions securely.

Can I delete my account?

Yes. Go to Settings > Delete Account to permanently delete your account and all data. This action cannot be undone.

What happens to my bookings if I delete my account?

All your bookings, payment methods, and profile data will be permanently removed. Skillink does not retain deleted user data.
